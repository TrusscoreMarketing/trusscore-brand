## How to use them (from SKILL.md Section 14)

- Real spaces, real installs, real life in progress — no staged or aspirational stock.
- No colour grading, tinting, or hue-shifting that misrepresents the product finish.
- People should look like users, not models.
- Follow the image orientation, overlay, and mobile-legibility rules in Section 14.

## Why not in git

Product photos are large, numerous, and re-shot over time. Git would keep every version forever and bloat the repo, and the skill does not need them bundled. Keep the library in the DAM / Drive and link it here.
