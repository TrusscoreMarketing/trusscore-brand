## Grading

- Aligned: on-strategy (leads with the right thing, current beliefs only), every fact matching the canon exactly.
- Drift: mostly right but soft. Vague positioning, a metric stated loosely, strategy implied but not contradicted.
- Misaligned (blocker): a retired offer or belief stated as live, a false company fact (wrong price, wrong metric, a dead domain, a departed teammate listed as current, an invented case study), an off-strategy claim, or content aimed at the excluded audience.
